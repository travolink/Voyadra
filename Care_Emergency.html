Stay safe and healthy while exploring Kerala with these essential tips.

Health Tips

  • Drink bottled or purified water.
  • Carry ORS and essential medicines.
  • Use mosquito repellent in monsoon.
  • Wear light cotton clothing.
  • Ensure travel insurance coverage.

Travel Safety

  • Use ride apps or prepaid taxis.
  • Always wear helmets and seatbelts.
  • Avoid isolated places at night.
  • Check weather alerts during monsoon.
  • Follow beach & backwater safety rules.

Natural Safety

  • Swim only in lifeguard zones.
  • Beware of strong currents in rivers and backwaters.
  • Avoid trekking during heavy rain.
  • Stay away from cliff edges.
  • Carry flashlight during forest or night trips.

Pharmacies & First Aid

  • Locate nearest pharmacies using maps.
  • Carry basic first aid kit.
  • Store medicines properly.
  • Keep contact of local doctors & hospitals.
  • Check expiry dates before use.
